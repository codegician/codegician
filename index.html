Profile

I was born on the island. My experience as an immigrant in the United States has been one filled with closed doors. By the time I was able to take advantage of the resources naturally available to citizens, it was time for me to seek a blue collar job and set aside any aspirations I had of chasing my dreams. The birth of my newborn daughter has rekindled the desire to build, not just a comfortable life for my family and I, but also to create a positive impact in the lives of my people. What I do from here on out, will be felt for generations to come.

I am self-taught in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Xd and InDesign, and I am actively expanding and cultivating my creative masteries by obtaining credentials in the field of Graphic and Interaction Design. Along with mastering graphic and interaction design, I diligently study full-stack software development at Floodgate Academy. My long term goal is to relocate to Africa and contribute to the efforts of uniting the continent and lifting it to heights once seen. Kids are the future. Investing my time and energy to teach them is the perfect vehicle to drive this change so for today and ever onward, I challenge myself to become as much of creative powerhouse as possible.
